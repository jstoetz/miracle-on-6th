About the Miracle on 6th Street Holiday Light Show

Experience a one-of-a-kind drive-up light display with synchronized music, larger-than-life decorations, and endless holiday fun.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Find answers to our most commonly asked questions about the Miracle on 6th Street Holiday Light Show.

  • What hours are you open? The Light Show runs from 6pm to 10pm nightly. We ask that if any of the businesses at the office park are still open, please be conscious of other cars and pedestrians.
